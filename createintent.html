Creating New Intent

Intents are grouped user sayings with a similar purpose. For example a user may say, "I want to order a cheese pizza and that may be labeled with an intent of Order Pizza

  • An Intent is the intention of the user, or the users, interacting with the chatbot. For example I would like to go to Goa’ Here the intent can be categorized as ‘travel’. Now consider this phrase :‘I want to go to Goa this Friday’.
  • For example :What are some popular places to stay in goa ; what are the famous places in goa ;what are the well-known places in goa
  • For example bot will reply : Carnival. A parade in Goa Fantastic Beaches.Palolem in South Goa ;Best Areas in Goa for Beaches ;would you like to book hotels?
  • EXAMPLE :-
  • User - How's the weather for today
  • Bot - It seems 35 deg celcius
